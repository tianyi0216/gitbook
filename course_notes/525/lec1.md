## Linear Optimization

A function $$f: \mathbb{R}^n \rightarrow \mathbb{R}$$ is linear if

1. $$f(x+y) = f(x) + f(y) \forall x, y \in \mathbb{R}^n$$
2. $$f(\lambda x) = \lambda f(x)$$ $$\forall x \in \mathbb{R}^n$$
